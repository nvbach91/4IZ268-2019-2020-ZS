Premier League Tweets

Get tweets related to Premier League match! Just select two teams and press the search button.

Your hashtag is: #

Premier League is the best football league in the United Kingdom. Every PL team has a three-lettered unique code. To make searching on social sites easier, PL officially tells people to use hashtags made from the team codes joined to one match code. This app is designed to get the match tweets from Twitter. Unfortunately Twitter basic search API does not provide tweets older than 7 days and the maximum total count of tweets you can get is 100.
Author: Martin Jelínek, student at University of Economics, Prague

This is a Twitter app made as final work for course 4IZ268 at University of Economics, Prague. To use it, you need to login with your twitter account.
