Welcome to Music Quiz

First of all, to play Music Quiz you need to have premium Spotify account.

How to play:

At the begining you need to choose playlist.
You can choose from existing ones or set your own using URI.
Keep in mind that if you pasted the URI into the appropriate form,
the URI playlist will be loaded, not the chosen above. As soon as you press START,
the playlist will load and the game will begin.

For each correct option you are given one point. There is also a count of correct answers given in a row.

Control:

Use the PLAY and PAUSE to control playback. The NEXT option will switch to the next song, but keep in mind that the winning streak will decline.
If you like the playing song you can add the song to your Liked Song playlist by ADD button.
For sound volume setting use SLIDER with Spotify logo above.

GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!
Let's Start
Pressing Let's Start button will redirect you to Spotify Authorization. After authorization you will redirect to Music Quiz page.
