    Welcome!

    • Start game by selecting pokemon to play for from top most box by doubleclick.
    • Unless you have pokemon to bid in your collection, click start button.
    • Otherwise you have to bid pokemon by doubeclicking on pokemon in your collection of your choice.
    • Once the game starts you will have 15s to guess 3 pokemons on pictures.
      • If you manage to guess 2 in limit, you get pokemon you played for.
      • If you manage to guess 3 you will get a random card of pokemon in addition.
      • If you won't be able to guess correctly more then once or until time pases, you loose.
      • If there is pokemon bid, you looses that pokemon.
    • Good luck and guess them all!
